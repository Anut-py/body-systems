Welcome to the Inner Workings of the Body! In this website, you will learn about all the organ systems in the human body. If you don't know how to use this website, you can try the tutorial, or you can look at the cards below.

Tutorial

Don't know where to start? Try the tutorial! In the tutorial, you will learn how to select an organ system and control the animations of an organ system.

Selecting an Organ System

To select an organ system, click on it in the menu on the left side.

Play/Pause Animations

To play/pause an animation, click the play button or the pause button.

Controlling Animations

You can skip to the start or end of an animation by pressing the rewind or fast forward buttons.
